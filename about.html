--- layout: null section-type: about title: "About our club!" --- ## About us Thank you for visiting our website! UHS Game Development Club, UniGDC in short, was founded to provide opportunity to experience game development process for future game developers. Our goal is to teach and learn how to work as a group. Everyone has potential to be a great game creator. Join us, and you can make awesome games with your own hands!
